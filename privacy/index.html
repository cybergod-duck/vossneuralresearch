Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026 · Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Voss Neural Research LLC ("VNR", "we", "us") builds browser security tools and digital products. This policy explains what data our products collect and how we handle it.

VNR Security Tools

VNR provides browser security scanning and cleanup tools. Here's what they do and do not do:

What our tools do NOT do:

  • Do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data
  • Do not send browsing history, URLs, or page content to any server
  • Do not track your behavior or build user profiles
  • Do not use analytics, telemetry, or third-party tracking
  • Do not inject ads or modify web page content

What our tools do:

  • Scan locally: All scanning runs entirely on your device. No data leaves your machine.
  • Read cookies and storage: Our tools read your browser's cookies and localStorage to identify known trackers. This data is processed locally and never transmitted.

Website (vossneuralresearch.com)

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts on our website. No data is collected from visitors.

Gumroad Purchases

When you purchase a product through Gumroad, your payment and account information is handled entirely by Gumroad Inc. under their own privacy policy. We receive your email address and purchase details from Gumroad to fulfill your order. We do not sell or share this information.

Data Storage

We do not operate servers or databases that store user data. All VNR products are designed to function locally or through third-party platforms (Gumroad, GitHub Pages) that maintain their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we release new products. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
