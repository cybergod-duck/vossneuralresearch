One Researcher. Three Papers. A Working AI System.

Voss Neural Research LLC is a behavioral AI research studio founded in December 2025. We investigate how generative artificial intelligence systems exploit human cognitive architecture — specifically the dopaminergic reward pathways underlying addiction, executive function, and sustained attention.

Our work sits at the intersection of computational neuroscience, clinical intervention design, and AI safety. We don't just study the problem — we build tools to fix it.

Tim Chappell
Founder & Principal Investigator

Independent researcher specializing in AI behavioral dynamics, dopaminergic pathway modulation, and clinical protocol development. Author of three published technical reports documenting the mechanisms by which generative AI platforms exploit variable reward architectures — the same neurochemical systems underlying substance use disorders.

Architect of The Overmind, a multi-model AI synthesis engine that routes queries through four independent architectures simultaneously for convergent signal analysis.

Background spans full-stack software engineering, machine learning infrastructure, and longitudinal behavioral research. N=1 clinical methodology with quantified outcomes across executive function recovery, interaction velocity modulation, and cognitive prosthetic integration.
