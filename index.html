Discreet Empirical Study of AI in Stimulant Use Disorder Recovery

About

Voss Neural Research LLC is a privately held Wyoming limited liability company (Filing #2025-001839586, EIN 41-3072036) dedicated to the empirical study of how AI systems influence behavioral and neurocognitive patterns in individuals recovering from stimulant use disorder.

Current focus: Longitudinal N=1 observation of AI-assisted self-regulation techniques, craving modulation, and reward pathway stabilization in a single subject with documented SUD history. All research via personal experimentation with commercially available AI tools—no external subjects, no clinical trials.
