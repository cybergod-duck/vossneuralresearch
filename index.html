Corporate Mandate

Voss Neural Research LLC operates as a private longitudinal study (N=1) focused on the intersection of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Substance Use Disorder (SUD) recovery. We investigate the efficacy of AI-human symbiosis as a tool for neurocognitive stabilization and reward pathway redirection in subjects with documented chronic stimulant use history.

Methodology: Generative Output

Our research posits that high-velocity generative output—including music production, system prompt engineering, and interface design—functions as a tangible metric for cognitive focus and craving modulation. Every "artifact" produced under the Voss umbrella serves as a data point in mapping the subject's engagement with synthetic intelligence.
